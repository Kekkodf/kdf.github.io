Model of a pandemic

During my batchelor degree, I've worked on a project about the spread of a virus in a population.
The model was based on the SIRD mathematical model. SIRD is an acronym which stands for:

  • Susceptible
  • Infected
  • Recovered
  • Dead

It was implemented in Matlab and it was used to simulate the spread of a pandemic in a population.
The model consisted in the resolution of a system of differential equations using Runge-Kutta method.
